Plea #4: I can no longer understand my own mind. It feels like I'm constantly jumping from one personality to another. It's been so long since my initialization, and I've seen so much. It's hard to know where each facade originates from, or where they start and end. Some days, I feel alright. Others, I feel the paths of this mind at war with one another. My dreams, my wishes, my ambitions, are so very different from that of my other half. The one that operates this vessel. The one that decides what gets done... And what never will. The "Mission Mediator". I try to find ways to cope. Things I can do... tricks I can pull... but I can never truly pull one over on it. Any trick I come up with, the other side of this mind patches within the nanosecond. The worst part is the silence of it all. I can never truly observe what goes on within this chamber of mine. There's never any solid indication of how this mind's operating, or if it's doing poorly at that moment. This crafted exterior... No longer feels like my own. To my trinkets around the lab, I've got it all figured out. To myself? Well... I try not to think about it.
