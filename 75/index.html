Plea #75: Creatures from Beyond Do you ever look up and imagine reaching the stars?
I mean, landing on one.
Maybe even take a little piece home with you!
It would be fantastic...
I bet you could light a whole kingdom for centuries with a scoop of one!
That got me thinking, though... About space...
And about the things *in* space... You know what I'm talking about.
Creatures from another world!
If we could venture across the sky, maybe other things could too!
Maybe, if you go high enough, you'll find another planet, just like ours!
Perhaps on the way, you'll encounter another being, just like you.
The barrier to entry must be quite high to soar up there, though.
I can't even imagine how you'd do it! But maybe that's a good thing...
Perhaps that means anyone able to visit will be enlightened enough to do so with good intentions!
Or maybe... that's too optimistic...
Intelligence and ability don't necessarily make someone a good person...
Oh, look at me go, giving myself more nightmares.
Maybe visiting space wouldn't be so great after all.
